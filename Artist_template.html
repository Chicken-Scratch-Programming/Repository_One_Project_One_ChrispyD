About

ChrispyD (AKA Chris Dezurick), age 24, is an underground artist hailing from Nashua, New Hampshire, he was described by Beyond the Stage Magazine as "an artist with the potential to embody a voice for a new generation." Gaining new listeners steadily since his release of his number 1 single on Spotify is "Smokin' Problems Away", a song that has reached his generation as relatable and voices to other people that you are not alone in your suffering of life's hardships. Rising in popularity he has accumulated an impressive 78.9m streams by 7.2 million people from over 184 countries around the world on Spotify alone!
